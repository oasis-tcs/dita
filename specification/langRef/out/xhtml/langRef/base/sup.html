<sup>

A superscript is a letter, number, or figure that is printed above than the line. It is frequently used in chemical and mathematical formulas; it also is used for footnotes.

Usage information

This element is part of the highlighting domain. Use this element only when a more semantically appropriate element is not available.

Formatting expectations

Although rendering is left up to implementations, processors typically apply render the contents of the <sup> element in superscript highlighting or placed higher in relationship to the surrounding text. Superscript text also is often a smaller font than the surrounding text.

Specialization hierarchy

The <sup> element is specialized from <ph>.

Attributes

The following attributes are available on this element: Universal attribute group and outputclass.

Example

<p>The power produced by the electrohydraulic dam was 10<sup>10</sup> more than 
the older electric plant.  The difference was H<sub>2</sub>O.</p>
