<sub>

A subscript is a letter, number, or figure that is printed lower than the line. It is frequently used in chemical and mathematical formulas.

Usage information

This element is part of the highlighting domain. Use this element only when a more semantically appropriate element is not available.

Formatting expectations

Although rendering is left up to implementations, processors typically apply render the contents of the <sub> element in subscript highlighting or placed lower in relationship to the surrounding text. Subscripted text also is often a smaller font than the surrounding text.

Specialization hierarchy

The <sub> element is specialized from <ph>.

Attributes

The following attributes are available on this element: Universal attribute group and outputclass.

Example

<p>The power produced by the electrohydraulic dam was 10<sup>10</sup> more than 
the older electric plant.  The difference was H<sub>2</sub>O.</p>
